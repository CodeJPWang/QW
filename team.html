✨ 团队介绍

We have a diverse and international team of mentors, whose core members include:

我们拥有一支多元化、国际化的导师团队，核心成员包括：

  • Strong STEM background: Our founders and lead mentors come from top universities like Stanford, Harvard, Berkeley, and Columbia, with rich research or industry experience.
  • STEM 背景深厚：公司创始人及核心导师均来自 Stanford、Harvard、Berkeley、Columbia 等名校，拥有丰富的科研或职场经验。
  • Mentorship support system: Every student is paired with a subject-aligned mentor, who guides them through school selection, essay writing, interview prep, and more — significantly boosting admissions success rates.
  • 导师制支持系统：每位学生均配备专业对口导师，全程陪伴选校、申请文书、面试准备等各环节，高效提升录取成功率。
  • Hands-on professionals: Our mentors include elites with experience at Wall Street, top research labs, and global consulting firms, especially insightful in STEM job market trends and competency evaluations.
  • 实战派专才：教师团队包括曾在华尔街、顶尖实验室、国际咨询机构工作或研究的精英，尤其在 STEM 相关岗位招聘趋势与能力评估方面有深刻洞察。

We embrace a “Mentor + Tech + Content” integrated philosophy, delivering high-value, all-in-one international education services.

我们秉持“导师＋技术＋内容”融合的理念，为学生提供一站式高价值国际教育服务。
